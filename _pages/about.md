I'm now a postdoc at the Technical University of Berlin, supervised by [Prof. Giuseppe Caire](https://www.bifold.berlin/people/prof-giuseppe-caire-phd.html). 


I received my PhD degree at Queen Mary University of London in 2023, under the supervision of [Prof. Cunhua Pan](https://radio.seu.edu.cn/2023/1025/c19937a469630/page.htm). I received my Master's degree at the University of Science and Technology of China in 2020. 


My research interests include massive MIMO, RIS/IRS, and Near-field Communications.

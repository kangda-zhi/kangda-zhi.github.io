About Me

I’m now a Murie Curie research fellow at Technical University of Berlin, supervised by Prof. Giuseppe Caire. I received my PhD degree at Queen Mary University of London in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Cunhua Pan. I received my Master’s degree at the University of Science and Technology of China in 2020.
